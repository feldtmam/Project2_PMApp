About

PMApp

PMApp is a feature-rich, Flask-based tool that allows businesses to organize projects, collaborate, schedule work, plan events, create task dependencies, upload and share documents, and track and resolve issues. Some of PMapp standout features include:

Features, Functionality and Advantages

▪ Task Management

PMApp helps you make projects more manageable by breaking them down into smaller steps and assigning them to team members. Each project can be broken down into milestones, task lists, tasks, and even subtasks. You can give each of these units start and end dates, descriptions, attach relevant documents to them and assign them. Their status can be updated as a percentage completed at any time so you can see their progress. You can see every aspect of a task that you have assigned, or that has been assigned to you in list form. Rather than having tasks spread throughout your email inbox, you can see them all in one place, in a clear hierarchical manner, with their whole history and relation to the larger project including the parent record.

▪ Charts and Reporting

In addition to helping you organize and track the progress of your tasks, PMApp helps you take all the information collected from the tasks making up a project to give you dashboards and reports that help maximize efficiency. Gantt charts and various other charts show a projects interdependent tasks on a grid to show you how much time is scheduled for each task, and which tasks must be completed before a dependent task can be started. Resource utilization charts gives you a bird's eye view of your employees' availability to see who has too much work, and who is free for further tasks. Task reports show you the status of a project's tasks, so you know what percentage of the whole project is done, and where you are in danger of falling behind. In addition, you can customize reports for your own specific needs.

▪ Collaboration Tools

PMApp seeks to make collaboration between those involved in a project easy. Users can post comments, share documents or export to Excel files. It allows you to see project developments on a timeline, so that you can see what other team members are doing daily. In future versions, will offer even more tools. PMApp will be fully integrated with Microsoft Office.
